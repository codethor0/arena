# Code of Conduct

ARENA is a security research project. Contributors are expected to act professionally, document claims carefully, and respect responsible disclosure norms.

## Standards

Expected behavior:

- Be precise.
- Be respectful.
- Stay within the project safety scope.
- Avoid unsupported claims.
- Avoid vendor harassment.
- Keep research evidence clean and reproducible.

Unacceptable behavior:

- Publishing secrets.
- Encouraging misuse.
- Expanding the project into real-world unauthorized attacks.
- Harassment or abusive conduct.
- Misrepresenting findings.
- Claiming affiliation with vendors without authorization.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may remove issues, pull requests, or contributions that violate the project scope or safety requirements.
